# Sample Staff Questions

These fictional questions were submitted before class.

## Privacy and uploaded files

- Can I paste a customer email into AI if I remove the person's name?
- Is it safe to upload a spreadsheet if it has employee ID numbers but not names?
- What counts as sensitive information?
- Are free AI tools different from approved work tools?
- Can AI remember something I pasted last week?

## Prompting

- What makes a prompt good?
- Do I need to write long prompts every time?
- Can I save a prompt and reuse it?
- What should I do if AI gives me an answer that sounds too generic?

## When not to use AI

- Should I use AI to decide whether a customer gets a refund?
- Can AI help with employee performance notes?
- Is it okay to use AI for legal language if I ask it to be careful?
- What tasks should always stay human?

## Email

- Can AI help me write a difficult email without sounding cold?
- How do I keep AI from making my emails too cheerful?
- Should I tell people when an email was drafted with AI?
- Can AI summarize a long email thread for me?

## Spreadsheets

- Can AI find mistakes in a spreadsheet?
- What if AI gives the wrong total?
- Can AI explain a formula in plain English?
- Is it safe to upload a CSV file?

## Jobs and review

- Will AI replace office jobs?
- How do I check whether an AI answer is true?
- Who is responsible if AI makes a mistake?
- How much should I edit an AI draft before using it?
