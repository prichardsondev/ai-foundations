Sample pre-meeting notes for class practice

Topic: Reducing repeated packaging label issues

Purpose:
Review recent label misprints and decide what follow-up is needed.

Notes:
- Packaging reported label misprints on three separate days.
- The issue appeared on both A shift and B shift.
- One note says the printer alignment was checked after shift.
- Another note says the setup checklist may not be clear enough.
- Assembly also reported missing part issues, but that may be a separate topic.
- Quality reported rework on April 5, but the note does not say whether it is related to labels.

Open questions:
- Is the label printer setup process the same on both shifts?
- Was the same product involved each time?
- Did the checklist change recently?
- Who owns the follow-up action?

Decision needed:
Decide whether to update the setup checklist, inspect the printer, or schedule a short refresher.
