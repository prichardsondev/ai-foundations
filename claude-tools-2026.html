Claude vs ChatGPT

Use this as a quick reminder that tool selection matters more than brand loyalty.

How Claude differs from ChatGPT

Quick framing: Claude is often associated with long documents, reasoning, code, and safety-first workflows. ChatGPT is often associated with image generation, voice, video, and a broader plugin ecosystem.

Note on beta tools

Claude for Excel, PowerPoint, Chrome, and Cowork are beta products as of April 13, 2026. Some links may redirect to broader Anthropic pages if a dedicated product page is not yet public.
